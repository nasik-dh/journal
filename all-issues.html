About Our Journal

Islamic Insight

Islamic Insight is an international, peer-reviewed cross-disciplinary journal dedicated to publishing original scholarship of exceptional quality on all aspects of Islam and the Muslim world. Inasmuch as it caters for a robust scholarly engagement with Islam in light of cutting-edge developments in the humanities and social sciences, the journal provides a forum for high quality research works that contribute substantively to contemporary discourse in Islamic studies in all its richness and complexities. The journal is very keen on articles, research communications, and book reviews that deal with Islam and the Islamic tradition in its many facets and manifestations and exploit perspectives and analytics that straddle disciplines and frontiers of knowledge.
